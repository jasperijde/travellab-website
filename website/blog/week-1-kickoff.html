Week 1: The Travel Lab Journey Begins

March 29, 2026 · Week 1

Welcome to the very first Travel Lab blog post! We're a team of students at Erasmus University, and for our Entrepreneurial Lab project we're building something we're genuinely excited about.

The Problem

Traveling to a new destination — especially a far-away one — comes with a lot of unknowns. Is it safe? What vaccinations do I need? Are there cultural customs I should be aware of? Currently, this information is scattered across government websites, travel forums, and blog posts of varying quality. It takes time to find, and it's easy to miss something important.

Our Solution

Travel Lab brings it all together in one place. We want to create a platform where travelers can quickly find reliable information about their destination — safety, health, and culture — without spending hours researching.

But we don't just want to be another information website. We also want to build a community where travelers can connect with each other, share tips, and even find travel buddies heading to the same place.

What's Next

Over the coming weeks, we'll be developing our concept further, talking to potential users, and building out the platform. We'll document everything right here on this blog, so follow along!

Stay tuned — this is just the beginning. 🌍
